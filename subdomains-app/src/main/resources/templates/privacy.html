Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

What We Collect

  • Email address — for login, verification, and service notifications
  • Subdomain handle — your chosen subdomain name
  • DNS record values — the records you configure
  • Curriculum progress — which lessons you've completed

What We Do NOT Collect

  • IP addresses
  • Browsing behavior or analytics
  • Location data
  • Any third-party tracking

Data Retention

Your data is held until you delete your account. Inactive subdomains (90 days no activity) are cleaned up with prior email notice.

Your Rights (GDPR & CCPA)

  • Right to erasure: Delete your account to remove all associated data within 24 hours
  • Right to access: Contact us to request a copy of your data
  • California users: You have access and deletion rights under the CCPA

Third-Party Sharing

We do not share your data with any third parties. DNS records are inherently public (that's how DNS works), but we do not sell, share, or analyze your data.

Contact

For privacy questions, contact us at privacy@codersfarm.com
