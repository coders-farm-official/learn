About Coders Farm DNS Manager

DNS Manager is a project of Coders Farm, a nonprofit civic technology organization. Our mission is to make the internet's infrastructure accessible and understandable to everyone.

Why Free Subdomains?

DNS is one of the most invisible and misunderstood parts of the internet. By giving people a real, working subdomain, we turn an abstract concept into something you can touch, own, and learn from.

Educational Mission

Every feature in the dashboard is paired with plain-language explanations. Whether you're a student, a hobbyist, or someone setting up their first home server, DNS Manager helps you understand what's actually happening when you type a URL.

Open & Nonprofit

This service generates no revenue. It is funded by Coders Farm's operational budget and is directly aligned with our 501(c)(3) educational mission.
